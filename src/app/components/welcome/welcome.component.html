Welcome to Tip Tap

Introduction

Practice typing using the QWERTY layout.

The Beginner and Intermediate lessons are composed from the English alphabet. Each lesson is 60 seconds in duration. Your are graded on a Words per minute (wpm) scale. In this context, a word is a random sequence of 5 characters from a given character set (e.g. [a, q, z]).

Finger Placement

Your index fingers, both left and right, should always find their way back to F and J. The F and J keys have small bumps on each key to easily identify them without looking down at your keyboard.
